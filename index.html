Ask Us Anything
How fast is payment after I send USDT?
Payment is processed within 3–5 minutes of your USDT transaction getting 1 blockchain confirmation. On TRC20 (TRON), usually under 2 minutes. On ERC20 (Ethereum), 5–10 minutes. We process immediately — no delays on our end.
What are the minimum and maximum trade limits?
Minimum trade is $20 USDT. Maximum per single transaction is $50,000 USDT. For amounts above $10,000, contact us in advance. VIP customers can trade unlimited amounts with prior arrangement.
What if my payment is delayed or not received?
If payment is delayed beyond 15 minutes after blockchain confirmation, contact us on WhatsApp with your transaction hash. We never keep your money — if there's an issue, we resolve it or refund you in full.
Is it safe to trade with Mr Coin?
Absolutely. Operating since 2020 with zero fraud cases. Every transaction is blockchain-verified and manually processed. We provide written trade confirmations for large trades. Our 10,000+ satisfied customers are our best proof.
Which USDT network do you support?
We support USDT on TRC20 (TRON) and ERC20 (Ethereum). We recommend TRC20 for faster, cheaper transactions. Always confirm the correct network before sending — wrong network can result in loss of funds.
Can I get a better rate for large amounts?
Yes! Trades above $500 USDT automatically qualify for VIP rates — often ₦5–₦20 above standard. The larger your trade, the better the rate. Contact us before initiating large trades.
Do you buy crypto (Naira to USDT)?
Yes, we offer Naira to USDT as well. Contact us on WhatsApp for the current buy rate. Minimum purchase is ₦50,000.
How do I know you won't disappear with my USDT?
We've built our reputation over 5 years with thousands of satisfied customers. We encourage you to start small on your first trade to build trust. Check our social media and community testimonials — we're here to stay.
